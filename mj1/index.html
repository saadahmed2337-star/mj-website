MICHAEL JACKSON

The Man • The Music • The Magic

1970s

The Prodigy

Born in Gary, Indiana, Michael was the heart of The Jackson 5. Even as a child, his voice and charisma outshone everyone. From "I Want You Back" to "ABC", he was a global sensation before he was even a teenager.

"I remember crying while doing my homework because I wanted to go out and play, but I had to go to the studio."

1980s

The King of Pop

In 1982, Thriller transformed the music industry forever. It won 8 Grammy Awards, introduced the iconic moonwalk to the world, and redefined music videos as cinematic experiences.

Later in the decade, the album Bad was released, and Michael turned every stadium performance into more than just a concert — it became a powerful and unforgettable emotional experience.

1990s

The Global Icon

The 90s were a time of evolution for Michael. With albums like Dangerous and HIStory, he continued to push musical boundaries while also using his platform to advocate for humanitarian causes. His music videos became mini-movies, and his influence on pop culture was undeniable.

Despite the controversies, his legacy as a musical genius and a global icon remained intact. He continued to inspire generations of artists and fans alike. His impact on music, dance, and culture is immeasurable.

2026

The Cinematic Return

The legend returns to the big screen. The highly anticipated biopic "Michael" is officially set for a global theatrical release on April 18, 2026. Directed by Antoine Fuqua, the film promises an intimate look at the man behind the legend.

Starring Jaafar Jackson—Michael’s own nephew—who perfectly embodies the King of Pop's voice, dance, and spirit. The film will cover his entire life, from the Jackson 5 era to his unrivaled global stardom, featuring over 30 iconic songs.
